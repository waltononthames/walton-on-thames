Welcome to Walton-on-Thames.org — a new independent guide to one of Surrey's most pleasant riverside towns.

This site is a passion project built by someone who loves this area and wanted a single, well-organised place where locals and visitors alike can discover the best of Walton-on-Thames, Hersham, and Whiteley Village.

## What you'll find here

**A visitor guide** — things to do, riverside walks, history and heritage, and practical information like how to get here by train, car or bus.

**An eat & drink guide** — restaurants, pubs, cafes and takeaways, with honest descriptions and verified contact information.

**A local directory** — a growing catalogue of local businesses, services, and community organisations.

**Events listings** — what's on across the three neighbourhoods, from the annual Walton Carnival to weekly markets and seasonal celebrations.

**Community news** — local stories, history features, and updates that don't always make the Surrey papers.

## How listings work

Every business listing on this site is researched using publicly available information and verified as accurately as possible. If you spot an error, or if you run a local business and want to suggest an edit or claim your listing, please [get in touch](/contact/).

This is an independent site, not affiliated with Elmbridge Borough Council, Visit Surrey, or any official tourism body. That independence means we can be honest and direct about what's worth visiting — and what isn't.
