Walton Rowing Club's annual regatta is one of the most enjoyable sporting events in the local calendar. Held over a weekend on the Thames at Walton, it attracts crews from clubs across the south of England competing in a range of categories from juniors to masters.

Spectating is free and you can watch from Cowey Sale and the riverside path. The atmosphere is convivial — deck chairs, a Pimm's or two, and the splashing of oars.
