Barrio Fiesta is billed as the **largest Filipino event outside the Philippines**, and it makes its home at Apps Court Farm on the Thames for 2026 — a full day of Filipino food, music, dancing, and cultural celebration.

Expect traditional Filipino cuisine, live performances, and the kind of warm, community-spirited atmosphere that has made Barrio Fiesta a fixture in the Filipino-British calendar. Families are very welcome — previous attendees have described it as an "excellent family day out."

**Getting there:** Hampton Court station is the nearest rail link, served by South Western Railway from London Waterloo. Buses 411, 461 and 555 serve the area. Parking on-site costs £10 per car and must be purchased in advance; disabled spaces are available via One Out parking.
