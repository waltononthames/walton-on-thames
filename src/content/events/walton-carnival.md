The Walton Carnival is one of the highlights of the local year — a free, family-friendly festival that brings the community together for a day of live music, entertainment, rides, and food.

The carnival typically features a procession through the town centre before gathering at the recreation ground for an afternoon of entertainment on multiple stages. Local schools, clubs, and businesses all participate.

**Practical information:**
- Free entry
- Bring cash for stalls and refreshments
- Dogs welcome on leads
- Nearest station: Walton-on-Thames (10 min walk)
