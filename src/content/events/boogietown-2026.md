Boogietown brings one of the summer's biggest line-ups to Apps Court on the Thames near Hersham — a full day of soul, funk, and disco with an extraordinary roster of headliners.

The 2026 bill features **Gloria Gaynor**, **Earth Wind & Fire Experience by Al McKay**, **Soul II Soul**, **Gabrielle**, **Shalamar**, **Maxi Priest**, **Heather Small**, **Boney M**, and **Gok Wan**.

Apps Court is a well-established outdoor events venue right on the riverbank, with easy access from Walton-on-Thames and Hersham. Shuttle buses and parking packages are available when booking.

**Tickets:** From £54.50 standard, £120 VIP — book via the official website. Payment plans available from £10.
