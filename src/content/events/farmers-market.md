Walton's farmers' market is a popular fixture in the town's social calendar, bringing together local producers and artisan makers for a morning market on Church Street.

Expect a good variety of seasonal vegetables and fruit, freshly baked bread and pastries, local honey, cheeses, free-range meat and eggs, and speciality preserves. A great opportunity to stock up on quality local food and have a chat with the people who grew or made it.

*Check local notice boards and social media for current market dates — the market typically runs on the first Sunday of the month.*
