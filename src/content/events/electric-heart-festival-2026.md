Electric Heart Festival takes over Apps Court Farm on the Sunday following [Boogietown](/whats-on/boogietown-2026/), making it a full festival weekend on the Thames at Walton.

Gates open at **2pm**, with performances beginning from 2pm through to **10:30pm**. Last entry is 6pm. The event is **strictly 18+** with Challenge 25 ID policy in effect.

**Getting there:** The nearest train stations are Walton-on-Thames and Hampton Court, both around 30–40 minutes from London Waterloo. A shuttle bus service runs from Hampton Court Station. Car parking is available on-site (Gates 1 and 3 for vehicles).

**On the day:** The festival operates a **100% cashless** payment system. No re-entry is permitted. Bags must not exceed A4 size (21cm × 30cm × 8cm). An accessible raised viewing platform is available for access ticket holders.

**Tickets:** Available via the official website and the Skiddle app — digital tickets only, no need to print.
