Ashley Park Road runs through what was once the private grounds of Ashley House, a Georgian mansion that stood at the centre of a large estate. The grounds are now partly public open space, offering a pleasant tree-lined walk very close to Walton town centre.

The parkland is a favourite with dog walkers and families, offering shade and greenery in an otherwise built-up area. The mature oaks, beeches and limes give a sense of the estate's former grandeur.

Ashley Park also holds a remarkable place in sporting history: in September 1749, Prince Frederick of Wales and the Earl of Middlesex played what is now recognised as the **first recorded game of baseball in history** on these grounds. A blue heritage plaque at the adjacent Walton Cricket Club on Ashley Road commemorates the event.
