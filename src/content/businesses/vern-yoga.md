VernYoga operates from the Centre for the Community on Queens Road in Hersham, offering both yoga and Zumba Gold — the lower-impact, accessible version of Zumba designed particularly with older adults and those returning to exercise in mind.

The combination of yoga and dance fitness under one instructor gives VernYoga a breadth that suits people who want variety in their weekly movement practice. The Queens Road venue — a well-established community hub — provides an accessible and welcoming setting.

**Zumba Gold:** A lower-impact, modified version of standard Zumba, set to Latin and international music. Highly inclusive — suitable for participants who want the energy and enjoyment of dance fitness without high-impact jumps or complex choreography.
