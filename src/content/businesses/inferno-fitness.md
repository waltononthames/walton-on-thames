Inferno Fitness has built a loyal local following at The Ember Centre in Hersham by combining proper gym facilities with the tight-knit community feel of a smaller independent operation. The breadth of services here is notable for a single venue: gym access and small group training alongside yoga and Pilates classes, and crucially, sports therapy — which means you can train and get treated in the same place.

The small group training format hits the sweet spot between the cost of personal training and the anonymity of a regular gym class: small enough that the coach knows your name and your limitations, group-sized enough to create a motivating energy.

**Sports therapy:** On-site sports therapy is available for treatment of injuries, soft tissue work, and training support — check the website for practitioner details and booking.
