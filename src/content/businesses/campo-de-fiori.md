Campo de' Fiori brings a genuine taste of Italy to Walton-on-Thames. Located on Church Street close to the town centre, it's a reliable choice for a relaxed dinner or a celebratory meal.

The menu focuses on classic Italian dishes — hand-made pasta, risottos, grilled fish and meat — made with quality ingredients. The wine list is predominantly Italian and well-chosen.

The atmosphere is warm and unhurried, with attentive but unpretentious service. Booking recommended at weekends.
