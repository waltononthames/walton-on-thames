Three Sixty Gym on Bridge Street covers a broader range of fitness needs than most single gyms in the area. Alongside the main gym floor and personal training services, the facility incorporates martial arts — with classes in various disciplines — and benefits from on-site physiotherapy provision, which is a genuine advantage for anyone managing an injury or wanting to stay in peak physical condition.

The combination of training and therapy under one roof means you can lift, train, and get treated without needing to travel elsewhere. It's particularly well-suited to athletes, those recovering from injury, and people who want coaching alongside their gym access.
