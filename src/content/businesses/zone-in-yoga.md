Zone In Yoga offers a thoughtful approach to yoga teaching across the Hersham and Walton area, with a timetable that reflects the rhythms of the year through seasonal flow classes. Ashtanga yoga — a structured, physically demanding practice built on a set sequence of postures — is the other main offering.

The option to attend in person or online gives Zone In Yoga a flexibility that suits both local residents and those who prefer to practice at home. The online offering maintains the connection to a real instructor rather than pre-recorded video.

**Styles:** Seasonal flow (a creative vinyasa approach) and Ashtanga. Check the Zone In Yoga website for current class locations and the online booking platform.
