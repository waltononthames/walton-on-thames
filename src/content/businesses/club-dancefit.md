Club DanceFit brings the best argument for never calling exercise "working out" — sessions are genuinely fun, high-energy, and based around dance choreography that gives you a proper cardio workout while you're too busy enjoying yourself to notice.

Classes are held at Three Rivers Academy on Hersham Road, in a large hall well-suited to the format. All fitness levels are welcome — no dance experience required, just a willingness to move and enjoy it.

**Good for:** Anyone who finds traditional gym sessions uninspiring and wants a fitness class that doesn't feel like a chore. A popular option for both regular exercisers and those returning to fitness.
