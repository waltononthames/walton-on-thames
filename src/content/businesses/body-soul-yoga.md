Body Soul Yoga offers a thoughtful, unhurried approach to yoga teaching — the focus is on depth of practice and understanding rather than a conveyor belt of drop-in sessions. Based in Berkeley Gardens, the offering includes structured courses (ideal for those learning yoga properly from scratch), workshops on specific themes or styles, and private one-to-one sessions.

The private session option is particularly valuable for beginners who feel intimidated by group classes, or for experienced practitioners looking to deepen their practice with individual feedback.

**Good for:** People who want to understand yoga properly rather than just attend classes; those who prefer a private or smaller-group setting.
