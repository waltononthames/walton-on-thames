Better Body Pilates runs mat Pilates classes from community venues in Hersham, including All Saints Church Hall and St Peter's Church Hall on Burwood Road. The church hall setting reflects the ethos: genuinely accessible, community-spirited fitness rather than a polished boutique studio experience.

Mat Pilates is an excellent foundation for anyone building core strength and body awareness, and the small class sizes typical of community-venue sessions mean you get a reasonable level of individual attention from the instructor.
