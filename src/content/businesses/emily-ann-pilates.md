Emily Ann Pilates offers private, one-to-one Reformer Pilates sessions — the kind of individual instruction that simply isn't possible in a group class setting. Working one-on-one with a specialist instructor means the session is built entirely around you: your goals, your injury history, your current ability, and the specific movements that will benefit you most.

Private Reformer Pilates is the gold standard for rehabilitation, for those new to the method who want to build a proper foundation before joining group classes, or for experienced practitioners who want to push into more advanced work.

**Best for:** Post-injury rehabilitation, bespoke goal-focused training, beginners wanting individual foundations, and those who prefer the privacy and pace of a 1:1 session.
