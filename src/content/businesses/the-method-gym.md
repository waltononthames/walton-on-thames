The Method Gym has built a devoted following in Walton-on-Thames by doing a specific thing very well: functional fitness, delivered with genuine coaching expertise and a community feel that stands apart from the anonymous gym experience.

If you want to move better, get stronger, and actually look forward to your training sessions, this is the place.

The community-driven approach means classes are coached rather than just instructed. Members tend to know each other, and the atmosphere skews towards accountability and encouragement rather than ego.

**Best for:** People who want structured coaching over a self-directed gym floor, and anyone training for functional fitness events or Hyrox competitions.
