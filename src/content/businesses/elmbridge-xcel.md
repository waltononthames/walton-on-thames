The Elmbridge Xcel is the anchor of fitness provision in Walton-on-Thames — a large, well-run community leisure complex operated by Places Leisure on behalf of Elmbridge Borough Council. It sits right on Waterside Drive beside the Thames, which makes it one of the more pleasantly located leisure centres in Surrey.

The facilities are genuinely comprehensive: a main gym floor with cardio and weights equipment, a 25-metre swimming pool with lane swimming sessions, multiple fitness studios running a full weekly class timetable (spin, aerobics, Pilates, aqua aerobics), a climbing wall, and sports hall facilities for badminton and other court sports.

It is the natural first port of call for families, beginners, and anyone who wants the most complete facility under one roof. Membership options range from monthly gym access through to family swimming packages.

**Opening hours:** Check the Places Leisure website for current session times — the pool and studios operate on a timetable rather than open access.
