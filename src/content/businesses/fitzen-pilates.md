Fitzen Pilates does one thing and does it well: Reformer Pilates. Unlike studios that offer Reformer alongside a broader timetable, Fitzen is purely apparatus-focused, which tends to mean tighter class sizes, more individual attention, and instructors whose sole expertise is in this method.

Reformer Pilates is particularly valued for its effectiveness in building deep core strength, improving posture, supporting injury recovery, and providing genuine low-impact resistance training. It's a strong choice for people who've been recommended Pilates by a physiotherapist, or who simply want something more structured than a mat class.

**Location:** On Hersham Road, convenient for both Walton town centre and the Hersham area.
