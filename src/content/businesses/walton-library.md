Walton Library is conveniently located within The Heart Shopping Centre, making it easy to combine a library visit with shopping or other errands.

The library offers a full lending service for books, audiobooks, e-books and DVDs, as well as free public computer access and Wi-Fi. Regular children's storytelling sessions and reading groups take place throughout the year.

**Note:** Library services are managed by Surrey County Council. For current opening hours, special events, and digital borrowing, check the Surrey Libraries website.
