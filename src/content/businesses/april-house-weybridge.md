April House is a small, carefully run guesthouse tucked away in Springfield Meadows — a peaceful residential street in Weybridge close to the town centre and station. Rooms are individually decorated with a boutique sensibility, and the welcome is personal in the way that independent B&Bs tend to be at their best.

This is the right choice for visitors who prefer the atmosphere of a private house to a chain hotel, and who appreciate the extra attention that comes with owner-run accommodation. Return guests are common here.

**Distance from Walton-on-Thames:** Approximately 2 miles. Weybridge station is within walking distance, connecting to London Waterloo in around 30 minutes.
