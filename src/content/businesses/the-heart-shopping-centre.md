The Heart Shopping Centre is the beating commercial heart of Walton-on-Thames, offering a modern, covered retail environment in the town centre.

With over 60 stores across fashion, beauty, homewares and food, The Heart serves both residents and visitors looking for a good range of high-street names. The Vue Cinema adds an evening draw, while the food court and surrounding restaurants mean it's a destination for a full day out.

The central atrium regularly hosts community events including the Walton Christmas Lights Switch-On, market stalls, and live entertainment.
