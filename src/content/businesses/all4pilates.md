All4Pilates takes a community-first approach: running mat Pilates classes from church halls and community spaces across Hersham (including Hersham Baptist Church on Arch Road and the Hersham Day Centre on Queens Road) and extending into Walton-on-Thames venues.

Mat Pilates — using body weight and resistance bands rather than reformer machines — is highly accessible and excellent for building core strength, improving posture, and increasing flexibility. The community setting and welcoming approach make this a good entry point for those coming to Pilates for the first time.

**Multiple venues:** Classes run across several locations — check the All4Pilates website for the current timetable, which covers both Hersham and Walton-on-Thames.
